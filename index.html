My Journey

From Accountant to Web Developer

My name is Arshad Khan, and I come from a non-technical background with a B.Com degree. But my real journey didn't start after graduation — it started during it.

While pursuing my graduation, I was also working as an accountant at an Asian Paints store to manage my fees and support my family. Balancing studies and a full-time job wasn't easy, but I never let go of my dreams. Deep down, I always had a strong interest in technology. I wanted to build something digital, something impactful.

Even though I wasn't from an IT background, I was determined to step into the tech world. I began learning Digital Marketing on my own — exploring tools like Meta Suite, Meta Ads, and Google Ads. Slowly, I started building websites using WordPress, turning small ideas into working platforms.

But I wasn't satisfied. I wanted more control, more creativity. That's when I took the leap into web development — learning HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. It opened a whole new world for me.

Today, with over 2.5 years of experience in Web Development, SEO, and Digital Marketing, I work on creating responsive websites, optimizing content for search engines, and helping brands grow online.

I didn't come from tech — I worked my way into it. And I'm just getting started.
